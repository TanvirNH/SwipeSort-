Features

Everything you need
to reclaim your camera roll

🎞️

Photos & Videos

Sort both photos and videos in the same app. Switch between modes with a single tap and apply the same swipe gestures to your entire media library.

📁

Custom Albums

Create as many albums as you need. Organize photos into categories like Vacation, Screenshots, or Family — exactly how you think.

↩️

Instant Undo

Made a mistake? Tap undo immediately after any swipe action to reverse it. Deleted items also go to Recently Deleted for extra safety.

🔍

Full-Screen Zoom

Double-tap any photo to inspect it at full resolution with pinch-to-zoom before deciding its fate.

📷

Live Photos

Long-press on a Live Photo card to watch it play in full-screen before you sort it.

📊

Sort Stats

Track your daily sorting streak, swipes today, this week, and your all-time total. Stay motivated to keep your library clean.

🔔

Sort Reminders

Enable weekly local notifications to remind you to stay on top of your growing photo library. Completely private — no server involved.

🔒

100% Private

Your photos never leave your device. No accounts, no cloud uploads, no tracking of your media. Your memories are yours alone.

📋

Bulk Select

Long-press in any album to enter multi-select mode. Move or delete multiple photos and videos at once for fast batch cleanup.
